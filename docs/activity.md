# Activity

The **Activity** view gives you a small completion history without turning belki into a reporting tool.

---

## What it shows

Activity includes:

- completed tasks today
- completed tasks yesterday
- completed tasks this week
- completed tasks this month
- all-time completed tasks
- current completion streak
- a 26-week completed-task heatmap

Below the overview, completed tasks are grouped by completion date.

---

## What counts as activity

A task counts when it is marked completed. Recurring tasks can also record completed occurrences; those occurrences appear in belki's completion history.

---

## Limits

Activity is intentionally lightweight. It is not a time tracker, productivity score, or analytics dashboard.
